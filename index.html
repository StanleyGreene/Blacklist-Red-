Raymond Reddington
About Him

He's a crime lord who's real origin is unknown. He's a criminal mastermind he was the 4th and later 1st on the FBI Most Wanted Fugitives. He suddenly turned himself in to the FBI after evading them for 20+ years. As a kid he was always intelligent, he was a star at the Navy academy and was being groomed for Admiral before he disappeared, he is constantly on the run and only care about business.

He's the FBI Most Wanted man and has control and eyes everywhere in the crime organizations and has taken down a lot of high targets the FBI didn't knew existed. Every move he makes is for his own interest every high target the FBI takes down he gets something out of it secretly.
